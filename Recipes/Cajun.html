Cajun Chicken Pasta

Rich and Creamy Cajun Chicken Pasta


If you're wanting something declicious to cook the family look no further it is right here. This delicious recipe will melt in your mouth!

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces linguine pasta
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 teaspoons Cajun Seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 4 fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to aa boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente 8 to 10 minutes. Drain

  2. Step 2

    Cut chicken breast into strips. Place chicken and Cajun seasoning in a plastic bag, Shake to coat.

  3. Step 3

    Melt butter in a large sskillet over medium heat. Add chicken and cook, stirring, until browned and almost cooked through, 5 to 7 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    Add bell peppers, mushrooms, and green onions. Cook, stirring, 2 to 3 minutes

  5. Step 5

    Reduce the heat and stir in cream, basil, lemon pepper, salt, garlic pepper, and black pepper. Heat through. Add cooked linguine, toss, and heat through

  6. Step 6

    Sprinkle with Parnesan and serve
