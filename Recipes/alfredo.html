Chicken Alfredo

A nice creamy and meaty Chicken Alfredo to end the Night


This recipe is a quick and easy make! Whenever you think you don't have the time to cook something that is nice and filling throw this recipe together sit back and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into chuncks
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 (16 ounce) packages thin spaghetti
  • 4 (15 ounce) jars of Alfredo Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Italian Seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons of Garlic Powder
  • 2 tablespoons of Chopped Onion
  • 2 tablespoons of Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of Onion Powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon of cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1 1/2 shredded Parmesan Cheese (optional)
  • 2 cups chopped broccolli (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Season Chicken with salt and pepper. Heat a large skillet over medium high heat. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, 7 to 10 minutes

  2. Step 2

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook thin spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    Meanwhile, add Alfredo sauce to the chicken along with Italian seasoning, garlilc powder, onion, basil, salt, black pepper, onion powder, and cayenne. Bring to a slight boil; stir in Parmesan Cheese. Cook mixture until cheese is melted and you are able to lift a spoon out of the sauce without cheese hanging onto it, 3 to 5 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer. Bring water to a boil. Add broccoli, cover, and steam until tender, 2 to 6 minutes. Add broccoli to sauce.

  5. Step 5

    Drain cooked pasta. Scoop into individual serving bowls and pour sauce on top
