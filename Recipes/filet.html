Filet Mignons With Pepper Cream Sauce


Want a nice and mouth watering steak? This is the recipe for you. Bring that five star steak home to your own kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup coarsely crushed black peppercorns
  • 4 (6 ounce) 1 1/2 inch thick filet mignon steaks
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/3 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Place the peppercorns into a shallow bowl. Sprinkle the beef tenderloin filets with salt on both sides, and coat boath sides with crushed peppercorns.

  2. Step 2

    Melt the butter with the olive oil over high heat in a heavy skillet (not nonstick) until the foam disappears from the butter. Gently place the steaks in the pan, and cook until they start to become firm and are reddish-pink and juicy in the center, about 3 1/2 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 130 degrees F (54 degrees C). Remove the steaks to platter, and cover tightly with foil.

  3. Step 3

    Pour the beef broth into the skillet, and use a whisk to stir the broth and scrape up any dissolved brown flavor bits from the skillet. Whisk in cream, and simmer the sauce until it's reduced and thickened, 6 to 7 minutes. Place the steaks back in the skillet, turn to coat with sauce, and serve with the remaining sauce.
